The Project

Africa suffers from a significant data gap across sectors, which limits the ability of governments, businesses, and social enterprises to make informed, data-driven decisions. Existing data is often outdated, fragmented, or incomplete. Here at Thorix_by_Kenny we look to build Africa's largest consent-driven, AI-powered data bank, enabling deep insights into social, financial, healthcare, and infrastructure needs, and driving equitable development across the continent.
